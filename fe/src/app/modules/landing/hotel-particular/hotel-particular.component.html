Tranquillity and serenity find their home in this Seychelles-island hideaway

Taking its name from the rest stops made by seafarers of the Ottoman Empire, L’Escale Resort Marina & Spa is a refuge for today’s travellers. Surrounded by rugged mountains, emerald forests, and multihued waters, today it’s a haven of natural beauty.

From the Indian Ocean to Morne Seychellois (Seychelles’ highest peak), enjoy breath-taking views of the island from the comfort of your private terrace or balcony. Luxurious and refined, details throughout the hotel bear the hallmark of Winch Design - renowned for their work on superyachts. Think wood panelling, cream-white furnishings and a splash of nautical memorabilia in the bedrooms. Lose yourself in the all-encompassing tranquillity of the rainfall shower, before settling down for the night in irresistibly soft cotton bed linen.

Enjoy your stay!
